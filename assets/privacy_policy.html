Privacy Policy

Stroke5 Keyboard — Last updated: 2024

Overview

Stroke5 Keyboard (“the App”) is a Chinese stroke-based input method for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how information is handled when you use the App.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No keystrokes or text you type are recorded or sent anywhere.
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are collected.
  • No internet connection is required or used by the keyboard at runtime.
  • No location, contact, microphone, or camera access is requested.
  • No advertising identifiers or device fingerprints are gathered.

How the App Works

All text processing, dictionary lookups, and candidate generation happen entirely on your device. No data ever leaves the device through this App.

Permissions

The App requires only the standard Android BIND_INPUT_METHOD permission, which is mandatory for any keyboard app to function. No other permissions are requested.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud services.
